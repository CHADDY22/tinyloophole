How much will my tattoo cost?
Most pieces can fall into four categories:

$300 - small, relatively simple tattoos

$400-500 - A palm sized, more detailed piece. Or a larger tattoo with little details.

$600-800 - Larger tattoos with high detail.

$1200+ - Any full day session.

Stomach tattoos never exceed $1200.

Backs fully completed are usually around $2500-3000.

I do work on a sliding scale basis, which you can read about here.
Do you require a deposit?
Yes! All appointments require a non-refundable $200 deposit. While traveling, I require a $250 deposit.

Deposits go directly to the cost of your tattoo.
When will I see my tattoo design?
Drawings are presented the day of the appointment. I do not send out drawings for any reason prior to the appointment.
When should I arrive for my appointment?
No need to arrive early for your appointment, I typically arrive at the time of your set appointment!
What should I bring to my appointment?
Please bring your ID, face mask, a bottle of water, headphones, and if you’d like- your favorite vegan snack! Please come alone to your appointment unless we’ve spoken about a guest coming along with you.
What will I need to heal my tattoo?
You will need a vegan non-scented balm and Dr. Bronner’s Peppermint Castile soap. You may also use any non-scented balm and soap of your choosing. I will give you  detailed healing instructions upon your appointment!
May I use numbing cream or spray at my appointment?
I ask my clients to NOT use numbing creams or sprays prior to their appointment but I am happy to apply a numbing spray during your appointment if need be.

For larger projects (stomach/chest/full back) I am happy to discuss numbing creams that you may use prior to your appointment. I want you to be comfortable but most of all, I want you to be SAFE.
What makes a tattoo vegan?
I use all vegan inks, tattoo care products, cleaning products, and razors. Some tattoo inks and tattoo products contain bone char, glycerin from animal fat, gelatin from animal hooves, or shellac from beetles - mine do not!
